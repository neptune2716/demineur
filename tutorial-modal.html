Basic Strategies

How to Play

The goal of Minesweeper is to uncover all cells that don't contain mines without detonating any mines.

  • Left-click to reveal a cell
  • Right-click to place a flag where you think a mine is located
  • The numbers indicate how many mines are in the adjacent 8 cells
Basic Gameplay
Left-click to reveal, Right-click to flag

First Click Strategy

Your first click is always safe! Try to click in the middle of the board to maximize your chances of revealing a large area.

Using Numbers

If a revealed number matches the count of adjacent flagged cells, you can safely reveal all unflagged adjacent cells.

Using Numbers
Numbers indicate adjacent mines

1-2-1 Pattern

Advanced Techniques

Probability Analysis

When you have multiple possibilities, analyze which cells have the lowest probability of containing a mine.

Speedrun Mode Techniques

In Speedrun mode:

  • Click on a number to reveal surrounding cells if the correct number of mines are flagged
  • Right-click on a number to auto-flag mines if the correct number of cells remain unrevealed
